Accelerating climate action by growing the green economy.

Announced during NYC Climate Week in September 2022 and led by Her Highness Sheikha Shamma bint Sultan bin Khalifa Al Nahyan, UAE Independent Climate Change Accelerators (UICCA) was created as an independent global entity to accelerate climate action across three priorities: circular economy, carbon solutions, and climate adaptation.

UICCA programmes and initiatives are designed to drive the UAE’s transition to a green economy through effective innovation, collaboration, and policy advocacy in support of the UAE Net Zero by 2050 strategic initiative. Our work focusses on fostering strategic partnerships, driving innovation, and providing expert advice to inform and advance climate action through research, policy, and capacity building. Through this collaborative framework, UICCA bridges gaps between sectors, aligns goals, and significantly drives collective impact on the global climate action agenda.

“Most experts agree on the importance of climate change but disagree on how to address it. As a result, sustainability efforts are fragmented and ineffective.

UICCA began with a question: what if we broke down silos and focused on collective action instead?”

— Her Highness Sheikha Shamma bint Sultan bin Khalifa Al Nahyan

President & CEO, UICCA

What We do

Launchpad

The Launchpad is designed as a catalyst for growth, specifically targeting green startups with climate technologies. By providing a dedicated pathway, we aim to fast-track these startups, helping them to navigate the complexities of scaling their operations within the UAE.

Our support ranges from offering access to funding and market opportunities to enabling mentorship from industry leaders.

Advisory

Leveraging our team's diverse global expertise, UICCA offers comprehensive advisory services for the private and public sector.

Through data-driven research, insightful policy analysis and development, and bespoke capacity building programmes, we empower and enable partners to fulfill their sustainability commitments.

Alliances

UICCA fosters collective climate action by establishing coalitions across public and private sectors, focusing on specific climate challenges. These coalitions amplify the UAE’s collective impact through shared expertise and resources, driving coordinated climate solutions and best practices within the UAE and globally.

Priorities

Our efforts are concentrated on three main priorities: boosting climate adaptation initiatives, accelerating the adoption of circular economy practices, and developing advanced Carbon Solutions. These focus areas are essential in reducing emissions and aligning with the UAE's commitment to achieving Net Zero by 2050.

01. Climate Adaptation

UICCA is dedicated to enhancing resilience against climate change through global collaboration. Our focus includes protecting infrastructure and ecosystems, and implementing strategies for a sustainable future, aligning with global climate adaptation efforts.

02. Circular Economy

UICCA advocates for a circular economy to reduce waste and improve resource efficiency across sectors. UICCA supports the UAE's Circular Economy Policy 2031, promoting sustainable manufacturing and consumption practices.

03. Carbon Solutions

UICCA supports the UAE's Net Zero 2050 goal with a focus on decarbonisation in power, industry, and transportation. We drive innovation in carbon capture and clean energy technologies, aiming for a significant reduction in emissions.
